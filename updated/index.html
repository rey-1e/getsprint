How to Customize Your Workspace

  1. 1

    Click the Puzzle/Extension icon in your browser's toolbar and pin LeetCode Sprint.

  2. 2

    Open the extension popup while viewing any page on leetcode.com or leetcode.cn.

  3. 3

    Click on any palette color dot inside the Select Theme grid. The background layers, borders, and margins of your workspace will transform instantly.
